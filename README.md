## üìä Key Insights

- Certain pizza categories consistently outperform others in revenue contribution.
- Large-size pizzas generate the highest sales value.
- Monthly sales trends reveal peak purchasing periods useful for planning promotions.
- A small group of top-selling pizzas contribute a large percentage of total revenue.
- Lower-performing items present opportunities for re-pricing or marketing strategies.

## üéØ Business Recommendations

- Focus promotional activities around peak demand months.
